Imagine your dreamhome.

What do you see? Maybe you see yourself sitting and having a drink in your newly bought sofa, looking out over the central of Stockholm. Or maybe you see yourself close to nature, opening a window and feeling the sun in your face. However, in process from dream to reality, you often occur bumbs in the road. On a website the realestate may look perfect and cost within your budge. However, when you finally go to visit it IRL, the reality may not match with the information you were provided when browsing. Info like, how close it is to nature, how much noise the neightbourhood generates, how polluted the air is and so on.


What if there was a service in which you could find not only the internal info concerning a recident, but also important external info? This is all possible with our new service! This service will give you a more complete picture of what you are looking at in order to help you find your dream home! 

Lets say you have found your dreamhome, AWESOME! In order to make this dream come true, you now might want to validate your financial position? Or find a service which calculates your economical situation in terms of taking a loans? Just navigate to the menu and click on the loan tab. This will quick and easily direct you to our accommodation costs page, which allows you to choose a bank and thereby endup on their financial pages.
